nature of inequality

Women get less opportunities in society today, and of those opportunities, many are easier, because of the stereotype that women are less capable than men. This is especially true in the Silicon Valley where many technological positions opened up due to the tech boom. Because men and women get judged at unequal levels the percentage of women working is dramatically lower than that of men.

Historical Roots

Gender inequality has been prevalent in society as far as history dates back. It is clear that there was some heavy discrimination, the female count of Nobel Prizes, as an example. In addition, women suffrage movements popped up because of the reason of discrimination and the want for more rights and freedoms. Even with the suffrage movements, there are still many traces of gender inequality, mostly caused some variation of a superiority complex.

Historical Records

Reshma Saujani is a prime example of a woman trying to attempt to solve this problem, though rather successfully. She started an organization called Girls Who Code, which aims to get girls exposed to computer science. It's actually relatively sucessful because almost all the girls are interested in starting new computer science projects and are planning on majoring/minoring in it in college! Even more so, this is a local project that aims to help girls around the Bay Area.
